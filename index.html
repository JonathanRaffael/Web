About Us

Established in 1972, Hung Kee Rubber Factory is a Hong Kong-based manufacturer, supplier, and exporter of customized silicone and rubber parts. With over 40 years of experience, we operate our factory in ShenZhen, China, spanning 12,500 sqm. Our facilities include a clean production workshop and a state-of-the-art testing laboratory.

As an ISO9001:2008 certified manufacturer, we produce products meeting FDA, NSF standards for the US, as well as other quality certifications like RoHS, LFGB, EU, and JFSL.

We are committed to delivering top-quality silicone tubes, seals, gaskets, and o-rings. Our products are used globally in various applications, including kitchenware, electrical appliances, toys, and car accessories.

Our mission is to build on our legacy of product excellence and forge mutually beneficial partnerships with our customers.
